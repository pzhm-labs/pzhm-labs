## 🧠 Expertise

My focus is on **Deep Learning systems** that work in production, not just notebooks. I specialize in:

- **Neural Architecture Implementation:** Building models from scratch to understand every layer, activation, and gradient flow.
- **Production ML Systems:** Taking models from research papers to deployable services with PyTorch and Docker.
- **Distributed Systems Fundamentals:** Learning how to scale AI workloads across infrastructure.

I learn by **reading code line-by-line** and **rewriting it myself**—so when I showcase a project, I can defend every decision in a technical interview.

Beyond pure engineering, I'm building a foundation in **OS internals** and **systems programming** to understand the layers beneath the models. My goal isn't just to use tools—it's to understand how they work and make them better.
